catalog_simulate¶

gammapy.scripts.catalog_simulate(outfile, nsources, max_age, ism_density, supernova_energy, radial_distribution, velocity_distribution, spiral_arms, overwrite)[source]¶

Simulate a catalog of Galactic sources.

Several spatial and velocity distributions are available and each source has associated PSR, PWN und SNR parameters.

Available radial distributions: CB98 : F06 : L06 : P90 : YK04 : YK04B :

Available velocity distributions: H05 : F06B : Bimodal pulsar velocity distribution - Faucher & Kaspi (2006). F06P : Distribution by Lyne 1982 and adopted by Paczynski and Faucher.
